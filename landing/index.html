How it works

Three steps to smarter shopping

MergeMarket's microservice engine scrapes, normalises, and ranks prices in real time so you always see the freshest data from every store.

1

Search any product

Type a product name or scan a barcode. Our config-driven scraper engine fires across hundreds of store endpoints simultaneously.

2

Compare live results

Within seconds, prices are normalised to a standard schema, ranked lowest to highest, and displayed with store details and price history.

3

Buy smart, save more

Go directly to the cheapest store, set an alert for a future dip, or wishlist the item and come back when the time is right.
